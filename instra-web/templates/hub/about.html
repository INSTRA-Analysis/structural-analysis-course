About

Structural Engineering, Taught & Tooled

InstraHub is a platform for structural engineers — bringing interactive learning, practical software, and services together in one place.

Mission

Why InstraHub Exists

Most structural engineering software is a black box: numbers go in, diagrams come out, and the why stays hidden. InstraHub exists to change that — starting with education and extending to the tools engineers use every day.

The platform is built by practising structural engineers, grounded in real project experience and academic rigour.

  • Apps — standalone engineering software, starting with StructLab. Available now →
  • Services — consulting and bespoke software. Coming soon.
